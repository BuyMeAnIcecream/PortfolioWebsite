Spellcasters VR
Online Multiplayer Magic Dueling

Built with the goal of creating a truly social online VR experience, Spellcasters VR takes the fantasy of magic duels, and turns it into a team based competitive experience. Each player chooses their role (attacker, tank, healer), and draws gestures with their wands to summon offensive or defensive spells. Strategizing in the heat of battle is a must, as each role has unique abilities that will greatly benefit their team members. This project supports multiplatform players using both the HTC Vive and Oculus Rift to connect and play together.

About

Hi! I'm Anton Starodub. I love people. However, human interaction is the most mysterious thing for me. I think, games simplify it a lot. They are the smooth tools of interaction, that disarm us, take away the sharp edges of our personality. They help us to focus on the process of interaction, rather than differences of the opinions or personalities. I'm the game designer, therefore, I'm the communication simplifier. Let's make people communicate! Let's make World a little better.
